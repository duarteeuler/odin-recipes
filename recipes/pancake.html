Pancake

Pancakes are a classic breakfast food enjoyed around the world. They are made from a simple batter of flour, baking powder, salt, sugar, milk, and eggs, which is cooked on a griddle or in a pan until golden brown. Pancakes can be topped with a variety of sweet or savory toppings, such as syrup, butter, fruit, whipped cream, or even bacon and eggs. They are easy to make at home and are a great choice for a quick and satisfying breakfast or brunch. Whether you prefer them thin and crepe-like or thick and fluffy, there's a pancake recipe out there for everyone.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Butter or oil
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, eggs, melted butter or oil, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  4. Heat a griddle or large pan over medium heat and coat with a thin layer of butter or oil.
  5. Drop spoonfuls of batter onto the griddle and cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve the pancakes warm with your choice of toppings.
